My map illustrates the population of 18 to 24 year olds with a highschool education or equivanet in North Carolina by county in 2020. Most of the counties in North Carolina have between 60 to 2971 people within the age of 18 to 24 that have a highschool education or equivalent. Orange county, where Chapel Hill is located, it one of the most educated areas for this age group in North Carolina.
